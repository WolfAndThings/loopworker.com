Case Study 01

A Smash Burger Restaurant

Bold flash photography. Zero photography budget. Full content launch in 7 days.

Challenge

A new restaurant opening with zero existing content. No photographer in the budget. No time for a traditional shoot. They needed to look like they'd been open for years on day one.

Solution

We built a complete brand system around a direct-flash, Portra 400 film aesthetic with a red and yellow palette. Bold, in-your-face food photography that feels like it was shot by a $5K-a-day photographer.

Results

From discovery call to fully automated posting in one week. The brand launched with a 30-day content calendar and auto-posts twice daily to Instagram. No ongoing effort from the owner.

Case Study 03

A Luxury Cave Resort

Cinematic architectural renders. Marketing-ready before the property even opened.

Challenge

A luxury resort in Southeast Asia was still under construction but needed a complete marketing package for pre-launch campaigns, investor decks, and travel platform listings. No property to photograph.

Solution

We generated cinematic architectural renders and lifestyle imagery using a Kodak Gold 200 aesthetic with warm pastels. Soaring vaulted ceilings, polished stone, brass fixtures. Architecture as the subject, guests small in frame.

Results

A complete pre-opening marketing package: 60+ renders across Instagram, website, and booking platforms. The brand launched across three platforms simultaneously, months before the doors opened.
