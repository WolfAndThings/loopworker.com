About AI Brand Photography

What is AI brand photography?

AI brand photography is professional-quality brand imagery generated with artificial intelligence, custom-tailored to your brand identity. It's not stock photos. Every image is built using real camera specifications, film stocks, and lighting direction to match the exact aesthetic your brand needs. Think of it as having a photographer, art director, and stylist — all working from your brand DNA.

Does AI photography actually look real?

Yes, when done right. The key is using film-grade aesthetics — real film stocks like Portra 400 and Kodak Gold 200, actual camera specs, and intentional imperfection. We avoid "hyperrealistic 8K" prompts because that's what makes AI images look fake. Our anti-AI philosophy produces images with film grain, natural light fall-off, and the kind of subtle imperfections that make photographs feel real.

Can I use AI photos for my website and social media?

Yes, you receive full commercial usage rights for all generated images. Every image is created specifically for your brand and is yours to use across your website, social media, advertising, print materials, and any other marketing channels.

What industries work best with AI photography?

Restaurants, e-commerce brands, hotels and hospitality, fashion labels, real estate, personal brands, and agencies all see excellent results. Any business that needs a high volume of professional visual content — and doesn't have $10K+ per quarter for traditional photography — benefits from AI brand systems.

About the Process

How long does it take?

7 days from discovery call to full delivery. That includes brand DNA extraction, visual system design, image generation, content calendar creation, and automation setup (for Growth and Scale tiers).

What do I need to provide?

Just show up to a 30-minute discovery call. We extract everything we need — your brand values, aesthetic preferences, target audience, content goals, and competitive landscape. If you have existing brand guidelines or inspiration images, that's helpful but not required.

How many revisions do I get?

Starter and Growth tiers include 1-2 rounds of revisions on the visual direction and individual images. The Scale tier includes unlimited revisions until you're completely satisfied with every detail.

Do you handle the posting too?

Yes. Growth and Scale tiers include full content automation setup. Your content posts to Instagram on autopilot — no daily effort from you. We build the calendar, schedule the posts, and set up automated publishing that runs without you touching it.
